What We Do

Reviresco Ltd provides independent clinical safety and digital risk consultancy services for digital health vendors and NHS organisations. We specialise in DCB0129/0160 compliance, ePR integration safety, and regulatory alignment across digital transformation programmes.

We work across the full system lifecycle — from initial concept and vendor onboarding through to live deployments, continuous improvement, and safety monitoring. Our consultancy combines regulatory knowledge with frontline clinical insight to help teams deliver safe, usable, and assured solutions.

Core Services

  • Clinical Safety Officer (CSO) Services – DCB0129 / DCB0160
  • Hazard Logs, Safety Case Reports, and CRMPs
  • ePR and third-party system integration risk review
  • AI auditability and explainability support
  • Digital transformation risk consultancy for NHS and vendors
  • Support with DTAC, DSPT, and NHS assurance readiness

Use Cases

  • Start-up vendor preparing for NHS procurement and DCB0129 sign-off
  • NHS Trust deploying a new EPR with embedded digital tools and FHIR APIs
  • ICS digital team needing retained clinical safety input across multiple workstreams
