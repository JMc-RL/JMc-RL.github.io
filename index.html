What We Do

Reviresco provides independent clinical safety and digital risk consultancy services for digital health vendors and NHS organisations. We specialise in DCB0129/0160 compliance, ePR integration safety, and regulatory alignment across digital transformation programmes.

We work across the full system lifecycle — from initial concept and vendor onboarding through to live deployments, continuous improvement, and safety monitoring. Our consultancy combines regulatory knowledge with frontline clinical insight to help teams deliver safe, usable, and assured solutions.

Core Services

  • Pre-hospital clinical expertise applied to digital risk consulting, with insight into urgent care workflows, triage systems, and ambulance service integration
  • Clinical Safety Officer (CSO) services including documentation, governance engagement, and independent sign-off under DCB0129 and DCB0160
  • System-wide risk analysis for digital health platforms, system integrations, configuration decisions, and data flows — including clinical workflow impact and interface reliability
  • Clinical safety input into AI, decision support tools, and explainability frameworks
  • Strategic risk consultancy across digital transformation programmes, clinical change, and NHS innovation projects

Use Cases

  • Start-up vendors preparing for NHS procurement and DCB0129 sign-off
  • NHS organisations planning or delivering complex digital programmes involving system rollouts, EPR enhancements, clinical workflow redesign, or integration of third-party tools and data flows
  • ICS programme teams seeking independent clinical safety oversight across ongoing digital initiatives, system change, and integration efforts
